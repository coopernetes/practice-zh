## Pros and Cons of existing apps

Pleco in particular is one of my favourite pieces of software if for no other reason than their
[commitment to ongoing support of their users](https://www.pleco.com/products/) as well as their pricing model. You pay
for the app and content add-ons but there is no subscription fees. Anyone who is interacting with the Chinese language
at all should have this app. It is well worth the money, the company stands behind their product, and it is doesn't cost
you a monthly fee.

The same I cannot say for other apps in this category (including HelloChinese). Many of them are subscription based and
require you to pay monthly fees to access content. This is not sustainable for many users, especially those who are
learning multiple languages or who are on a budget.

## Can't you just use Anki or other existing apps?

Anki is a wonderful app. However, I find that flash cards are not a great study device for myself. I want something that
generates sentences & phrases so that I can increase my comprehension.

I also want the ability to tailor my learning for my ongoing progress as well as test myself on phrases that I'm
starting to pick up from my native Chinese colleagues & friends.
